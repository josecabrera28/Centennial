Bug Smasher Game

Instructions:
Welcome gamer! Are you fast enough to catch the bug to the last level?? You need to click on the bug, and with each bug you smash you will score 1 point extra. There are three levels (red/yellow/green), once you get to the next one you will get a message on your screen and your score button will change to the respective color. If it gets too hard for you, you can always reset the game from the reset button. Enjoy it!!
